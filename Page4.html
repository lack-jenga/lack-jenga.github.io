Northrop Grumman Sector: Mission Systems

Northrop Grumman Mission Systems specializes in software, primarily for cybersecurity-related and communications-related tasks. The sector's headquarters are located in Linthicum, Maryland.

Most of my internship at the mission systems corps revolved around network engineering. For example, I worked with PuTTY to set up virtual LANs for various assignments, and I've also used NetApp technology to manage stored data.
